Themes/causes present in the report:

1. Equipment Inspection: The master inspected the launching equipment before the operation commenced, including the wire slings, recovery hook, and high-pressure piping of the hydraulic davit.

2. Wire Sling Failure: One of the four wire slings attached to the lifeboat parted, followed by the remaining three in quick succession, causing the lifeboat to fall several meters into the water.

3. Crew Restraints: The third officer had removed his restraints in preparation for disembarking before the lifeboat fell to the water, resulting in a head injury. The able-bodied seaman suffered a minor injury to his thumb. The remaining two crew members were unharmed.
