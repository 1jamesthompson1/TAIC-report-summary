Themes/causes present in the report:
1. Inability to determine the definitive cause of the fire due to disturbance of evidence during clean-up efforts by the crew.
2. Lack of effective command and control of the firefighting response, including failure to establish a command center and conduct crew muster.
3. Failure to contain the fire within the cabin until firefighting teams were fully ready to engage.
4. Failure to shut down the air supply to the cabin where the fire was located, allowing the fire to spread.
5. Ineffective firefighting drills and lack of familiarity with tasks and responsibilities in the event of a fire.
